# Deep Learning Theory or (in Pursuit of Unexaplainable)

In recent years, we have seen enormous progress in deep learning, but our theoretical understanding of why it works so well is still limited. This repository is dedicated to exploring the theory behind deep learning, with a focus on reproducing and understanding results from theoretical deep learning papers. The ultimate goal is to gain a deeper understanding of the existing theory, and to use that understanding to generate new insights and ideas for future research.

Inspiration sources:
- [There Will Be a Scientific Theory of Deep Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21691)
- [Learning Mechanics Project](https://learningmechanics.pub/)
